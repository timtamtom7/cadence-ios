## Description

### Short Description (170 chars)
> Cadence helps you build focus habits through structured sessions, streaks, and team accountability. Find your flow.

### Full Description

```
Dive into deep focus.

Cadence transforms your work sessions into mindful rituals. Built formacOS, it brings the flow state within reach — right from your menu bar.

🌊 STRUCTURED FOCUS SESSIONS
Set your duration, hit start, and let Cadence guide you. Pomodoro-style sessions with full menu bar control — start, pause, and track without switching windows.

🔥 STREAKS THAT STICK
Build momentum with daily focus streaks. Each completed session adds to your streak. Miss a day? Start fresh and rebuild.

👥 FOCUS TOGETHER
Team up with friends for shared focus sessions. Hold each other accountable and celebrate milestones together.

📊 SIMPLE PROGRESS TRACKING
See your focus history, weekly summaries, and trend insights — all without the noise.

🔔 SMART NOTIFICATIONS
Gentle reminders at the right moments. Never intrusive, always helpful — because context matters.

🎯 DEEP-SEA AESTHETIC
A calm, focused environment designed to reduce visual noise. Teal glows on dark depths. Built to disappear into your workflow.

---

Whether you're tackling a creative project, studying, or building something — Cadence meets you at the surface of deep work.

Find your flow.
```
