## App Store Listing

### Title
**Cadence - Focus & Deep Work**

### Subtitle
*Find your flow. Focus with friends.*

### Description
Cadence is your AI-powered focus companion that transforms deep work into a shared ritual.

**Key Features:**
- 🎯 **Smart Focus Timer** — Deep work sessions with personalized duration recommendations
- 🎧 **Ambient Soundscapes** — 15+ curated sounds to boost concentration
- 🤝 **Focus Partners** — Pair with accountability partners for mutual deep work
- 📊 **Focus Analytics** — Track your streaks, patterns, and weekly insights
- 👥 **Team Sessions** — Group focus time with your team
- 🤖 **AI Coaching** — Get personalized session recommendations
- 📅 **Calendar Sync** — Schedule focus blocks directly to your calendar
- 🏆 **Achievements** — Earn badges for consistency and milestones

**Why Cadence?**
Unlike guilt-driven productivity apps, Cadence creates a warm, inviting space for focus. No judgment, no pressure — just you, your goals, and your flow state.
