# Cadence — Round 12: Social Focus

## Theme
**Social Focus**

## Goals
- Partner matching and focus sessions with buddies
- Leaderboard with weekly rankings
- Team challenges and group streaks
- Share achievements and focus sessions

## Features
- [ ] Partner status display in menu bar (available/focusing/idle)
- [ ] TeamView integration with CadenceMac
- [ ] Shared focus sessions (two users, synced timers)
- [ ] Leaderboard showing weekly focus time
- [ ] Share session results via native share sheet

## Technical
- MatchingService for partner discovery (mock data in R12)
- TeamService for team challenges
- Session sharing via macOS share extensions

## Notes
Social features should enhance accountability, not add pressure. Focus on collaboration over competition.
