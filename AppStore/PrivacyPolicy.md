# Cadence Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2026**

## Data Collection
Cadence stores all session data locally on your device. Your focus sessions, streaks, and preferences are stored locally using SQLite.

## No External Servers
- All data stays on your device
- No account required for core features
- No ads or tracking

## Premium Features
- Optional subscription for premium features
- Processed through Apple In-App Purchase

## Calendar Integration
- Optional calendar sync for session reminders
- Creates events in a dedicated calendar

## Contact
For privacy concerns: privacy@cadence.app
